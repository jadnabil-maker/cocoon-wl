HOST
THE PRODUCT

A social media app for events.

Host is a sleek, elegant social media app designed to simplify and elevate how people plan, manage, and attend events. Whether you're throwing a party, organizing a padel match, or attending a brand experience in a new city, HOST connects people through shared experiences and real-time event engagement.

HOST
CORE FEATURES

What makes HOST different.

Twelve flagship features that power the entire platform, from registration and live event radar to AI-driven concierge experiences.

Registration & login
Phone and email verification, two-step codes, country selection, forgot password recovery.
User profiles
Personalized profiles with bio, photo, hosted history, attended events, social circles.
Event creation
Title, date, cover image, Google Maps integration, virtual links, RSVP rules, dress code.
Guestlist system
Custom guestlist folders like Family, Padel Squad, or Work that get attached to events instead of inviting people one-by-one.
Smart notifications
RSVP alerts, ETA from host, geofencing nudges, engagement pings on guestlist reactions.
Circles
Group chats, private events, polls, and verified hubs. Think Reddit meets WhatsApp for your scene.
Explore page
Land in any city and instantly see public events nearby, filtered by category and trending status.
Social heat map
Live event radar showing where your city is heating up, based on check-ins, reactions, and foot traffic.
Memories
Collaborative threads, memory maps, year-in-review recaps, venue loyalty badges.
Host score & verification
Reputation system based on guest feedback, completion rate, and engagement. Top hosts unlock Super Host status.
AI concierge
Generate events, suggest optimal times, send themed reminders, auto-create checklists.
Emergency mode
Safety-first reporting that alerts the host, admins, and trusted contacts when needed.
